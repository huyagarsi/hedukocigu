PHOTOS: See More Levi Johnston Playgirl Pics!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 4, 2024

Two new sexy shots from Levi Johnston's Playgirl shoot hit the Web Saturday, and show the 19-year-old Wasilla, Alaska, dad sprawled out giving his best bedroom eyes — but he's not totally nude.

There are 25 photos in the full Playgirl.com set — available with a paid subscription — with more images to be released later this week. All but six are shot in black and white.

On Tuesday, the first of Johnston's shots for the skin mag was released, showing him posing in a steamy shower.

Though Johnston — whose son, Tripp, with Sarah Palin's daughter, Bristol, was born last December — was expected to go full frontal during the shoot, a rep for Playgirl tells Usmagazine.com it never happened.

"Although Playgirl is ecstatic with the footage of the Levi Johnston shoot, and viewers are going to be pleased and surprised, Levi did not go completely full frontal, as was suggested by his camp a few weeks ago," Playgirl rep Daniel Nardicio told Us.
