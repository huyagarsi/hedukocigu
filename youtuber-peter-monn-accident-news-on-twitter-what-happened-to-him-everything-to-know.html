YouTuber Peter Monn has been involved in a horrific accident leaving one of the drivers dead. Monn is currently hospitalized. Follow the article to find out what happened.

Let’s keep Peter Monn in our thoughts and prayers cause he is fighting for his life after suffering from a horrific accident.

YouTube star Monn, who is hugely popular among fans for his take on several pop culture issues and his regular vlogs was revealed to be a person involved in an accident on Allisonville Road.

Let’s find out the details of the accident.

YouTube: Peter Monn Accident News On Twitter

Peter Monn is a YouTube star whose news of an accident has been surfacing on Twitter lately.

Monn, who was traveling on his grey jeep Grand Cherokee found himself being caught in a fatal crash at around 12:30 pm on February 16, 2022.

The crash which is remained under investigation at the moment happened on Allisonville Road between 126th Street and Orchard Boulevard, Fishers, Indiana.

The crash involved 3 vehicles, a tan Chevrolet 1500 pick-up truck, a brown Nissan Altima, and a gray Grand Cherokee jeep where Monn was traveling.

As of now, the witness had revealed that Monn’s jeep was traveling Northbound on Allisonville Road when it traveled left of center striking the pick-up truck and Nissan Altima.

The case is being investigated by Fishers Police Department.

Now with all the news of the accident running on online platforms netizens on Twitter have poured their thoughts and prayers on YouTube star Peter Monn and other people involved in an accident.

Is Peter Monn Okay? Let’s Find Out

Peter Monn, 49, is okay and is placed in a hospital after the accident. He is expected to recover.

However, Brent Jameson from Anderson, Indiana has died in a crash. The 47-year-old man was the driver of a Chevrolet 1500 pick-up truck.

Other victims who are hospitalized along with Monn are 26-year-old Devon Detling and Abdirizak Hassan. Both of them were passengers.

Sharmarke Jama, 46, driver of Nissan is safe and is not hospitalized.
