Kim Zolciak Looks Gorgeous In Throwback Bathing Suit Shot At The Pool

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, June 21, 2024

The reality TV star took to social media to share a new post that showed her in a pink bathing suit posing while her body was submerged in water. The photo ended up being both seductive and very ethereal so it makes sense why she liked it so much.

Kim Zolciak shared the image which was apparently a teaser from an older shoot for her skincare line Kashmere.

It seems like Kim has a lot more free time while in quarantine so she has been looking through her old photos.

So when she stumbled upon this one, she just had to post it!

The pic was not only a bikini shot but also a glam one since she had her hair in an elegant up-do while posing in a swimming pool.

The fact that her bikini body was mostly covered by water, paired with her hairstyle, made the image into something much more artistic and glam as opposed to a normal summer body shot.

In the caption, the former Real Housewives of Atlanta star wrote: ‘Love this pic! I have some great eyes, great hearts and good souls making sure we ‘Got’ the shot! Love these 2 @kroybiermann and @mrdblanks.’

https://www.instagram.com/p/B-0ho1eDR8-/

Of course, it looks like she shouted out her hubby and the photographer, both of whom can be seen in the shot as well, their backs turned to the camera.

As mentioned before, the photo is a throwback to her launch of her skincare line back in 2016.

At the time, she proudly announced on Don’t Get Tardy that ‘I have finally launched my dream, Kashmere is my new skincare line. torn between doing a lemon-y, clean scent or more of a stripper scent.’
