Who Is Skyler Gisondo?

Skyler Augustus Gisondo (born July 22, 1996) holds the spotlight as an American actor, recognized for his captivating performances in movies such as Licorice Pizza, Booksmart, and Vacation.

He has played compelling roles on TV shows like The Righteous Gemstones and Santa Clarita Diet.

Gisondo’s acting journey commenced at a tender age of six when he made his debut on television.

His mother, situated in Southern California, kick-started his career by submitting his photos to talent agencies. This ultimately led to an impactful audition for a nationwide Pizza Hut commercial.

Subsequently, Gisondo’s repertoire expanded to encompass a multitude of commercials, TV series, and films.
