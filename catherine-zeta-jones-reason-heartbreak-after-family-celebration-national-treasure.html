Catherine Zeta-Jones' reason for heartbreak soon after family celebration

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 15, 2024

The Oscar-winning actress' exploring days on Disney+ have come to an unwitting end

    Catherine Zeta-Jones' time in historical fiction may have just come to an end, as one of her latest projects just met a sudden end.

    According to Deadline, it was announced that Disney+ would not be renewing for another season.

    The series was a continuation of the popular film franchise which centers around a network of treasure hunters who travel all around the country to protect important artifacts in American history.

    The show is "an expansion of the movie franchise told from the point of view of young heroine Jess, a DREAMer in search of answers about her family who embarks on the adventure of a lifetime to uncover the truth about the past and save a lost Pan-American treasure."

    Also starring in the now canceled series were Lisette Olivera, Zuri Reed, Antonio Cipriano, Jake Austin Walker, and Jordan Rodrigues.
