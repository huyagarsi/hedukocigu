Jeff Goldblum Height, Weight

He is of American nationality and of Austrian-Jewish and Russian-Jewish ethnicity. He has been able to keep his body fit despite his height of 6 feet 4 inches and weight of about 89 kg.

Jeff Goldblum Education

In terms of education, Jeff first attended Taylor Allderdice High School in Pittsburgh. He attended Carnegie Mellon University after graduating from high school. Moreover, he has been interested in acting since he was a child. He also participated in drama during his university years.

Later, he relocated to New York to further his career. There, he enrolled in Sanford Meisner’s acting class at the “Neighborhood Playhouse.” He could also play the piano.

Career

His first film as a home-invading thug, “Death Wish,” was released in 1974. Then, in 1975, he starred in the television film “Columbo: A Case of Immunity.” In 1993, he appeared in the film “Jurassic Park,” which launched his career.

In addition, in 2006, he founded a new theater company called “The Fire Dept.” As a result, on May 28, 2018, he formed his own band, “The Mildred Snitzer Orchestra.” Similarly, on November 9, 2018, he released his debut single, “The Capitol Studios Sessions.”

Furthermore, the Disney show “The World According to Jeff Goldblum” was announced at the D23 Expo. The film, which stars Goldblum, will be released on November 12, 2019.

Jeff Goldblum Wife, Marriage

Jeff Goldblum has been married three times. On July 4, 1980, he married actress, Patricia Gaul. They divorced after six years, however. He married actress Geena Davis on November 1, 1987. They also divorced on October 17, 1990. Then, in 1995, he married Laura Dern, from whom he divorced in 1997.

Later that year, in 2014, Jeff married Olympic gymnast Emilie Livingston. On November 8, 2014, they married. Furthermore, on July 4, 2015, the couple gave birth to a son.
