Paula Deen: 25 Things You Didn't Know About Me

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Chef Paula Deen, 67,  serves it up for Us Weekly revealing 25 surprising things you may not have known.

1. I have a couch in my bathroom instead of a tub.

2. I don't like goat cheese.

3. I love to paint and make my own shell art.

Photos: Model body painting competition

4. I'm addicted to chewy ice and have a chewy ice maker in my garage.

5. I do deep-fry my bacon.

6. I love to have family, friends, and pets pile up in the bed and watch TV.

7. I have my iPad with me 24/7 and play games on it.

8. I will not leave my house without my fake eyelashes.

9. I cannot live without Diet Coke

10. I like to freshwater fish.

11. I have my own worm bed when I go fishing.

12. I jump whenever I see a rat or mouse.

13. I can't walk in heels.

14. I have three pet goats.

15. My all-time favorite meal is steak and potatoes.

16. Every once in a while I like to treat myself to an at-home massage.

17. I love to shop for clothes at Goodwill.

18. I enjoy eating peanut butter toast for breakfast.

19. I hate burgundy.

20. My favorite vacation is to the Atlantis resort in the Bahamas. 

21. I really don't like walking in sand.

22. I love to travel with my husband, Michael Groover.
