Julianne Hough's Sisters Sharee and Marabeth Are Gorgeous!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Runs in the family! Julianne Hough’s older sisters Sharee and Marabeth are just as gorgeous as she is. The Dancing With the Stars judge brought her siblings to Cosmopolitan's 50th Birthday Celebration at Ysabel in West Hollywood, Calif., on Monday, Oct. 12.

Sharee opted for a red jumpsuit while her sister Marabeth donned a floor-length lace black dressand rocked a red lip. Julianne, 27, showed off her dancer’s legs in a short black miniskirt, paired with a white lace top. Her new engagement ring from fiancé Brooks Laich completed her sexy yet demure look.

Julianne’s sisters have been in town visiting the Footloose star since the Primetime Emmy Awards on Sept. 20. Last month, Julianne and Sharee took a workout class by celebrity trainer Simone De La Rue.

“Thank you @bodybysimone for our KickA$$ workout!! You are the best!!! Love having my sissy @shareewise here too… Good workout buddy cause we are both so competitive! :),” she captioned an Instagram with her sis. 

Julianne also has another older sister named Katherine and, of course, an older brother, DWTS pro Derek Hough.
