Who Is Kavi Ladnier? Wikipedia

Kavi Ladnier is an actress. 

She will be appearing on the upcoming television series “The Sex Lives of College Girls”. 

Despite the fame, her biography has not yet been featured on Wikipedia. However, her information is available on some other sites on the internet. 

According to her LinkedIn., she earned her degree in Bachelor of Fine arts from New York University’s Trish School of Arts.  She is also a company member of Theatre Tribe and is currently studying with Stuart Rogers, the award-winning artistic director of Theatre Tribe in North Hollywood.

After living in New York City for 13 years, she then moved to Los Angeles in 2005. 

She made her guest appearance as Mira Shenoy, Mohinder’s ex, on Heroes. She also appeared in Las Vegas, and in two feature films: Mr. Woodcock (with Billy Bob Thornton and Susan Sarandon) and as a principal actress in Karma Calling directed by Sarba Das.

Likewise, she has done two national commercials for Chevrolet with her daughter Leela.
