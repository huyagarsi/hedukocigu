Benji Maddens sweet tribute to Cameron Diaz on their seventh anniversary -Kidspot

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 29, 2024

Singer Benji Madden has shared a sweet tribute to wife Cameron Diaz on their seventh wedding anniversary. 

Good Charlotte singer Benji Madden has posted the sweetest tribute to his wife, actor Cameron Diaz, on their seventh wedding anniversary.

Posting a photo of a tiger painting, the happy husband gushed about his strong marriage.

“Making time go slower”

Madden, who is known for posting sweet messages about his wife, wrote:

“Today 7 years married. Always dreamed of having a family like this.

“Happy, peaceful, consistent, loyal and unconditional equally filled with passion and depth. Calling it a Honeymoon doesn’t cover it. Real life.”

The loved-up singer added; “The only real challenge we have is making time go slower cause life goes by fast in the happy lane.

“Always and forever knowing we are home @camerondiaz Happy Anniversary. I love you!”

Cameron’s response

The comments section was quickly filled with congratulations, and Diaz herself commented too.

“I ✨❤️✨YOU MORE AND MORE EVERYDAY. Happy, peaceful, consistent, loyal and unconditional. Passionate and deep = OUR LOVE. LOVE YOUR FOREVER AND ALWAYS.”

Madden’s brother Joel added, “Y’all are the Goats”, meaning greatest of all time.

Australia’s own musician Ben Lee also wrote, “Beautiful.”

A long history 

Diaz and Madden tied the knot in a secret ceremony in 2015. Three years later, Diaz confirmed that she had retired from acting.

They welcomed their daughter, Raddix, two, in late 2019.

During a chat on the podcast, Anna Faris is Unqualified, in September 2021, Diaz opened up about meeting Madden for the first time through his twin brother Joel and his wife Nicole Richie. 

“I met mine through my now sister-in-law and brother-in-law, I met them first and then they didn’t set us up but we were in the same room because of them, and then we found each other,” she shared. 

“I was like, ‘How come I didn’t see him before?'”

When asked if the connection she had with Benji was influenced by any connection she had with Joel, Cameron was quick to point out the differences between the two. 

“They’re not the same, they’re so different,” she said. “Even though they’re twins, they’re very, very different obviously.” 

Cameron then went on to elaborate on just how immediately attracted she was to Benji.

“I saw him walking towards me and I was like, ‘Huh, he’s hot, I haven’t seen him before,'” she revealed. 

“But then when I saw him, like who he was, that’s what made me really be like, ‘Oh you, you’re special, you’re the guy, you’re the hidden gem in my life.'”
