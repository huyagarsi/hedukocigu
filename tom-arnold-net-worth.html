Early Beginnings to Stardom

Born in the small town of Ottumwa, Iowa, Tom Arnold‘s story is one of classic American resilience mixed with a healthy dose of humor. With a challenging childhood marked by his mother’s departure and his own battles with autism, Arnold’s early life was no laughing matter.

Yet, it was these trials that perhaps sharpened his wit, leading him to the University of Iowa, where his comedic talents began to take center stage.

Arnold’s big break came through none other than Roseanne Barr, leading to a whirlwind of professional and personal adventures. His role on Roseanne and subsequent marriage to its star catapulted him into the public eye, a place he’s remained through various career highs and lows.
