Alyson Hannigan: What's In My Bag?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Alyson Hannigan’s haul is family-friendly. “I’m always throwing in a change of clothes and this and that for the kids and me,” the mom of daughters Satyana, 6, and Keeva, 3, tells Us. The How I Met Your Mother actress and star of the Jockey Skimmies video series shares what else is in her Chanel tote.

Touchy Subject

“Sometimes, depending on how the sinks work at bathrooms, it’s easier to spray my girls’ hands. So I’ve got a spray bottle of natural hand sanitizer from CleanWell.”

PHOTOS: Random Friend Alert

Lip Fix

“I do have my one makeup product, the Clinique Chubby Stick in Bountiful Blush. My lips get dry, so I use that.”

Child’s Play

“I have Saty’s Strawberry Shortcake doll and a cow toy that my mother-in-law gave Keeva. You wind it up, and it does the splits and waves!”

PHOTOS: Happy Hannigan

Nuttin’ To It

“I’ve got a packet of Justin’s Almond Butter for a snack. It’s so yummy. I bring it for me, but my girls usually take it!”

Clean Read

Thank You!

You have successfully subscribed.

“I just finished reading The Life-Changing Magic of Tidying Up, by Marie Kondo, this morning. It’s basically about how to declutter and only surround yourself with what you need. It’s going to change my life! I’m very excited to be getting rid of most of my things.”

PHOTOS: Ballerina Satyana

More of what’s in Hannigan’s bag: white Valentino wallet, Louis Vuitton credit card holder, 10 Toysmith mini markers in a plastic case, My Little Pony grab-and-go coloring book, LaLaLoopsy doll, Kiva’s white woven bracelet with three pink and green rhinestone balls, hairbrush, rubber bands, Kleenex, Smith’s Strawberry scented lip gloss tin, Tic Tacs, Listerine breath strips, Jennifer Meyer nameplate necklace, Prada tortoise-shell sunglasses, iPhone, Gloves in a Bottle lotion, and kids-size Skimmies slip-shorts for her daughters in white and pink.
